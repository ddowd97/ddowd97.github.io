1) Players take turns placing one word at a time on the Dabble board.
2) Players may pass their turn, if they so choose.
3) Words placed on the board must be defined in the current dictionary.
     a) Placed words must not, as a consequence, form other words that are not defined in the current dictionary.
4) The first word placed must use the center of the board.
5) Words placed on the board must be connected to all other previously placed words.
     a) Placed words must either EXTEND, HOOK, or be PARALLEL to previous words.
6) The game ends when the score limit [if specified] is reached or when six consecutive passes occur.

1) A bonus space (DL, TL, DW, TW) is only used one time - when a new letter is placed on it.
     a) DL = Double Letter, TL = Triple Letter, DW = Double Word, TW = Triple Word
          i) The center of the board is a DW bonus space.
     b) Letter bonuses are applied before word bonuses - word bonuses come last!
     c) Word bonuses are applied to all words directly formed by that placed tile.
          i) Word bonuses are stackable - 2 DW ("Double-Double") is a x4 bonus, 2 TW ("Triple-Triple") is a x9 bonus!
2) Using all 7 tiles in one turn is a BINGO and awards 50 extra points (after bonus space calculation)!
3) No points are deducted when passing a turn.
